# Chapter Tagging Workflow (Genuine & Evidence-Based)

## Step 1: Fresh Start for Every Chapter
Har naye chapter se pehle main:
- `English M.CG.pdf` ko dobara analyse karta hoon.
- `21st Century Skill.pdf` ko dobara analyse karta hoon.
- Kabhi previous chapter ki tagging ya memory par depend nahi karta.
Isse har chapter independent aur unbiased analyse hota hai.

## Step 2: Complete Chapter Reading
Tagging shuru karne se pehle main poora chapter beginning se end tak padhta hoon:
- Wonder Window
- Peek Inside
- Main Lesson
- Learning Through Interaction
- Reflect and Respond
- Put on Your Thinking Caps
- Dictionary & Vocabulary Skills
- Grammar Lab
- Connect and Communicate
- Competencies and Beyond

Purpose ye hota hai ki chapter ka objective aur context samajh aa jaye.

## Step 3: Ignore Printed Tags
Book mein jo Competency ya 21st Century Skill already printed hoti hai, main use kabhi trust nahi karta. Har activity ko fresh evaluate karta hoon.

## Step 4: Analyse the Student Task
Main heading ko nahi dekhta. Main ye dekhta hoon ki student actual mein kya kar raha hai:
- MCQ solve kar raha hai?
- Reason de raha hai?
- Creative writing kar raha hai?
- Dictionary use kar raha hai?
- Discussion kar raha hai?
- Research kar raha hai?
- Grammar rule apply kar raha hai?

Tagging sirf isi action ke basis par hoti hai.

## Step 5: Match with Official Competency
Ab activity ko `English M.CG.pdf` ke official competencies (`C-1.1` to `C-5.3`) se compare karta hoon.
- Sirf official competencies use hoti hain.
- Koi nayi competency create nahi karta.
- Koi force fitting nahi karta.
- Jo competency sabse accurately student task ko represent karti hai, wahi assign hoti hai.

## Step 6: Match with Official 21st Century Skill
Uske baad activity ko official 12 skills se compare karta hoon:
1. Critical Thinking
2. Creativity and Innovation
3. Communication
4. Collaboration
5. Information Literacy
6. Media Literacy
7. Technology Literacy
8. Flexibility and Adaptability
9. Leadership and Responsibility
10. Initiative and Self-Direction
11. Productivity and Accountability
12. Social and Cross-Cultural Interaction

Unofficial skills ka kabhi use nahi hota.

## Step 7: Cross Verification
Tag final karne se pehle main verify karta hoon ki:
- Competency actual student task se match karti hai.
- Skill bhi wahi represent karti hai jo activity demand karti hai.
- Kisi aur competency ya skill ka better match to nahi hai.
- Agar better match milta hai to pehla tag reject kar diya jata hai.

## Step 8: Evidence-Based Explanation
Har tag ke saath main batata hoon:
- Competency क्यों?
- Skill क्यों?
Har explanation activity ke actual task par based hoti hai, na ki heading ya theme par.

## Step 9: Final Tag Output Format
Har activity ka final output isi format mein diya jata hai:
- Correct Competency
- Correct 21st Century Skill
- Competency क्यों?
- Skill क्यों?
- ✅ Final Tag

---
### Important Rules
- Har chapter fresh analyse hota hai.
- Previous chapter ki tagging reuse nahi hoti.
- Printed tags par kabhi trust nahi kiya jata.
- Sirf official competencies aur official 21st Century Skills ka use hota hai.
- Theme ke basis par kabhi tag nahi diya jata.
- Heading ke basis par kabhi tag nahi diya jata.
- Student ke actual task ke basis par hi competency aur skill assign ki jaati hai.
- Har final tag evidence-based aur reference documents ke saath cross-verified hota hai.
